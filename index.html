about_

I'm a PhD candidate in AI Security, jointly at Ikerlan and Radboud University. My research sits at the intersection of machine learning and security. I design backdoor attacks that expose how models break, and probe the limits of the defenses meant to stop them.

Before the PhD I earned an MSc in Computational Engineering & Intelligent Systems and a BSc in Computer Engineering (CS specialization), both at UPV/EHU.

001

Adversarial ML
